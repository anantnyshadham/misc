# Portugal 2026 — Dinner Picks 🇵🇹
### For family review · 8 nights · Jul 25 – Aug 1

**The approach:** every spot is **à la carte** (no 3–4-hour tasting menus), works for a **table of 9** including the kids, has **genuinely good vegetarian options** (not afterthought salads), leans **authentic Portuguese or high-end "ethnic,"** and is **high-end food / relaxed vibe** — not stuffy. Most tables seat **~7pm** (Portugal's kitchens don't open earlier). Each night has a **first pick → backup**.

**Our table:** 9 people · 4 kids (3, 8, 10, 12) · 8 eat fish/seafood · 1 lifelong vegetarian (her real veg option is called out at each spot).

## Sat Jul 25 · Sintra / Cascais — arrival night

**① Maré – José Avillez** · Portuguese seafood · ~€70–100pp · ~15 min from hotel
José Avillez's ocean-front Cascais restaurant: charcoal-grilled fish, clams Bulhão Pato, carabineiro & crab rice, cataplana — **plus meat dishes** (sirloin, veal) for anyone not doing seafood. "Sophisticatedly relaxed," two rooms + sea terraces, seats 9.
🥦 **Veg:** proper salads + vegetable small plates (Avillez does them well, though it's seafood-led).
🔗 https://marejoseavillez.pt/en/

**② Backup — Arola** · creative tapas · on-resort at Penha Longa (no drive)
Sergi Arola's casual tapas — shareable, kid-friendly, zero travel if we're wiped from the red-eye.
🔗 https://www.penhalonga.com/en/dining/

## Sun–Thu Jul 26–30 · Algarve — all on-resort at Pine Cliffs
*No night driving with the 3-year-old — everything's walkable on property, all ~7pm.*

**🌟 Rohini by Little Miss India** · Indian · the veg anchor
Authentic Indian with a Portuguese twist (Goan fish curry, tikka) and **abundant real vegetarian dishes** — the single best night for our vegetarian. Summer cultural dinners with music & dance.
*(Seasonal pop-up — we're confirming it's running our exact dates.)*
🔗 https://www.pinecliffs.com/en/dining/

**Yakuza by Olivier** · Japanese
One of the Algarve's top Japanese restaurants — open-air Zen garden, relaxed and informal. Sushi, sashimi, tempura, plus veg sushi / veg tempura / spring rolls / vegan salads.
🔗 https://www.pinecliffs.com/en/dining/yakuza-algarve-by-olivier/

**O Pescador** · seafood
Billed as some of the best seafood in the Algarve — contemporary beach restaurant, fresh-caught fish of the day.
🔗 https://www.pinecliffs.com/en/dining/

**Maré at Pine Cliffs** · casual Portuguese
Beachfront over Praia da Falésia — fresh seafood + grilled fish & meats + Mediterranean, cocktails & sangria. The easy, low-key night.
🔗 https://www.pinecliffs.com/en/dining/

*5th night = repeat the table favorite, or Piri Piri Steak House for a meat night.*

## Fri Jul 31 · Lisbon — authentic Portuguese

**① Bairro do Avillez – Páteo** · Portuguese · ~€50–70pp · Chiado (central)
Avillez's lively courtyard hall: traditional Portuguese done with care — seafood, fish, meat, knockout desserts — à la carte and made for sharing. Genuinely family-friendly, seats 9 easily, open every night. The wider "Bairro" also has a pizzeria and a tavern under the same roof if the kids want pizza/pasta.
🥦 **Veg:** real veg plates here, plus pizza/pasta next door.
🔗 https://www.bairrodoavillez.pt/en/pateo/

**② Backup — Prado** · modern farm-to-table · ~€55pp · Mouraria
Daily-changing, ingredient-driven modern Portuguese in a converted fish factory — **"many vegetarian options," good for groups.** The strongest veg of the Portuguese options.
🔗 https://pradorestaurante.com/

## Sat Aug 1 · Lisbon — high-end ethnic

**① Ibo** · Mozambican–Goan–Portuguese · ~€40–60pp · Cais do Sodré (riverfront)
Stylish waterfront room: crab curry, lamb xacuti, Mozambican prawns à la Laurentina, coconut-milk fish for the 8 — **plus many real veg options** (Indo-African vegetable cooking) for the 1. Large space, seats 9 easily, sunset river views. Open Saturdays.
🔗 https://www.ibo-restaurante.pt/

**② Backup — Sabores de Goa** · Goan / Indo-Portuguese · Anjos
Authentic Goan, proven with **big family groups (16–20)**, does **veg + pescatarian** beautifully — vindaloo, xacuti, cafreal, samosas. Warm, family-run. Book ahead for a 9-top.
🔗 https://saboresdegoa.com/en/

**③ 2nd backup — Zaafran** · refined North Indian · Saldanha
Often called Lisbon's best Indian — an India → Mozambique → Portugal flavor route, more elevated/intimate, with a vegetarian tasting option. Mon–Sat dinner. No website — reserve by phone: +351 213 558 894 (Largo Dona Estefânia 7).

## 🥦 The veg logic (on purpose)
8 of us eat fish/seafood; 1 is a lifelong vegetarian. The **Indian and Japanese nights** (Rohini, Yakuza, Ibo, Sabores de Goa, Zaafran) are where her food is genuinely *great* — full-flavor, not an afterthought. On the pure-seafood nights (Maré, O Pescador) she still has real veg plates, just fewer. That balance is deliberate.
