I

Sintra

Time budget: ~1.5 blocks — Jul 25 PM (arrival half-day) + Jul 26 AM (one block before the drive south).
Sights & activities
Pena Palace

Pena Palace

KEPT
The fairytale red-and-yellow hilltop palace — storybook magic, unreal views.
Shuttle up + earliest timed entry so the 71-yo & 3-yo skip the steep climb and the crowds.
Cascais

Cascais

KEPT
Flat, easy seaside town: promenade, beaches, ice cream.
The low-effort arrival-day win after the red-eye; right by the Maré dinner.

Quinta da Regaleira

OPTIONAL

Stair/tunnel-heavy (Grandma + 3-yo do the gardens) and a lot to add before a 3-hr drive.
Sintra National Palace

Sintra National Palace

OPTIONAL
The twin-cone palace in town — the flattest, most accessible of the three.
A gentler alternative if anyone wants a sight on arrival day.

II

Algarve

Time budget: 5 days, capped at 1–2 full outing days + at least one all beach/pool day; most days = a half-day outing + beach/pool.
Sights & activities
Benagil Sea Caves + dolphins

Benagil Sea Caves + dolphins

KEPT
The iconic golden cave with the sky-hole, plus summer dolphins — a kid highlight.
Whole group on a stable catamaran, or split for a kayak/small boat into the cave. Morning = calm + cool.
Ponta da Piedade / Lagos

Ponta da Piedade / Lagos

KEPT
The Algarve's most spectacular cliffs + charming, flat Lagos town.
Clifftop is easy for all; the 200-step descent (or a grotto boat) is the split-worthy part.
Praia da Marinha

Praia da Marinha

KEPT
One of Europe's most beautiful beaches; clifftop boardwalk views.
Views easy for all; the stairway down to the sand is steep. Pairs with Benagil.
Silves Castle

Silves Castle

KEPT
Red-sandstone Moorish castle over a sleepy river town.
Walkable ramparts, less crowded — a good culture half-day. Go in the morning.
Vilamoura marina

Vilamoura marina

KEPT
Flat promenade, boats, gelato — an easy all-ages evening.
Low-effort; pairs with a beach/pool day.
Pine Cliffs beach & pools

Pine Cliffs beach & pools

KEPT
The resort itself: cliff-top pools, km of golden-cliff beach, Porto Pirata kids' village.
The backbone of the Algarve leg — chill days, kids' club, no driving.
Cabo de São Vicente / Sagres

Cabo de São Vicente / Sagres

OPTIONAL
The southwest tip of Europe — dramatic 'end of the world' cliffs + lighthouse.
~1h+ each way past Lagos, very exposed/hot — a long day for the 71-yo + 3-yo. We lean cut.
Albufeira Old Town

Albufeira Old Town

OPTIONAL
Lively, touristy old-town evening.
A fun alternative to Vilamoura for the easy evening.

III

Lisbon

Time budget: ~1.5 days — Jul 31 PM (half-day after the drive) + Aug 1 (full day). Aug 2 is departure only.
Sights & activities
Oceanário de Lisboa

Oceanário de Lisboa

KEPT
World-class aquarium — giant tank, otters, penguins; flat, indoor, air-conditioned.
The best all-ages, beat-the-heat activity — the 3-yo and the 71-yo both win.
Belém: Jerónimos + Tower + tarts

Belém: Jerónimos + Tower + tarts

KEPT
Flat riverside icons + the original Pastéis de Belém custard tarts.
Book Jerónimos ahead; photograph the Tower from the gardens and skip its narrow interior climb.
Castelo de São Jorge + Alfama

Castelo de São Jorge + Alfama

KEPT
Panoramic hilltop castle over the old quarter.
Taxi/tuk-tuk up (Alfama's cobbles are steep); keep the wandering light.
Time Out Market

Time Out Market

KEPT
Huge food hall — something for everyone incl. veg + picky kids.
Lunch, not dinner.
Parque das Nações

Parque das Nações

OPTIONAL
Modern riverside + cable car, right by the Oceanário.
An easy, fun add for the kids.
Praça do Comércio / Baixa

A flat stroll — easy to fold into the Belém/center day.
