Algarve

Time budget: 5 days. Resort time by day: Jul 26 ~0–1h (Pena + drive; ~3h if Regaleira skipped) · Jul 27 ~4–5h PM · Jul 28 ~10h — the only full resort day · Jul 29 ~5h PM · Jul 30 ~2–3h late PM · Jul 31 zero (checkout). Sights & activities
Benagil Sea Caves + dolphins

Benagil Sea Caves + dolphins

KEPT
The iconic golden cave with the sky-hole, plus summer dolphins — a kid highlight.
Whole group on a stable catamaran, or split for a kayak/small boat into the cave. Morning = calm + cool.
Ponta da Piedade / Lagos

Ponta da Piedade / Lagos

KEPT
The Algarve's most spectacular cliffs + charming, flat Lagos town.
Clifftop is easy for all; the 200-step descent (or a grotto boat) is the split-worthy part.
Praia da Marinha

Praia da Marinha

KEPT
One of Europe's most beautiful beaches; clifftop boardwalk views.
Views easy for all; the stairway down to the sand is steep. Pairs with Benagil.
Silves Castle

Silves Castle

KEPT
Red-sandstone Moorish castle over a sleepy river town.
Walkable ramparts, less crowded — a good culture half-day. Go in the morning.
Vilamoura marina

Vilamoura marina

KEPT
Flat promenade, boats, gelato — an easy all-ages evening.
Low-effort; pairs with a beach/pool day.
Pine Cliffs beach & pools

Pine Cliffs beach & pools

KEPT
The resort itself: cliff-top pools, km of golden-cliff beach, Porto Pirata kids' village.
The backbone of the Algarve leg — chill days, kids' club, no driving.

III

Lisbon

Time budget: ~1.5 days — Jul 31 PM (half-day after the drive) + Aug 1 (full day). Aug 2 is departure only.
Sights & activities
Oceanário de Lisboa

Oceanário de Lisboa

KEPT
World-class aquarium — giant tank, otters, penguins; flat, indoor, air-conditioned.
The best all-ages, beat-the-heat activity — the 3-yo and the 71-yo both win.
Belém: Jerónimos + Tower + tarts

Belém: Jerónimos + Tower + tarts

KEPT
Flat riverside icons + the original Pastéis de Belém custard tarts.
Book Jerónimos ahead; photograph the Tower from the gardens and skip its narrow interior climb.
Castelo de São Jorge + Alfama

Castelo de São Jorge + Alfama

KEPT
Panoramic hilltop castle over the old quarter.
Tuk-tuk up through Alfama — the ride is part of the fun, and it's the only real way in: the lanes are too steep/narrow to self-drive or park near. Everything else in Lisbon is self-drive, own cars, car seat included.
Time Out Market

Time Out Market

KEPT
Huge food hall — something for everyone incl. veg + picky kids.
Lunch, not dinner.

Parque das Nações

An easy, fun add for the kids.
