# Portugal 2026 — Dish Guide, Allergy Notes & Booking Instructions

**Built for:** 1 shrimp/crustacean allergy in the group, 1 lifelong vegetarian.
**Read this first:** Shrimp-allergic people are very often reactive to *all* crustaceans (prawn, langoustine, crab, lobster) due to shared tropomyosin protein — those are flagged ⚠️ below. Mollusks (clams, mussels, octopus, squid, oysters) are a separate allergy category and usually fine for a shrimp-only allergy, but confirm with whoever has the allergy before assuming.

## Sintra / Cascais

### Maré – José Avillez (Cascais) — Jul 25 PICK
Upscale seafood-forward, José Avillez's hometown restaurant.

**Top 3 (non-shrimp):**
1. **Lombo de Corvina** — sea bass loin, olive oil & lemon
2. **Polvo ao alho** — charcoal-grilled garlic octopus
3. **Escabeche de fígado de bacalhau** — cod liver, vinegar-marinade style (starter)

**⚠️ Avoid:** Arroz de Carabineiros / Gambas (shrimp rice), Scarlet shrimp dishes, Royal Seafood Platter — all shrimp-heavy, the menu's signature dishes are largely built around it.
**Vegetarian:** Weak. Bread, salads, maybe a vegetable side — call ahead and ask the kitchen to prepare something.
**Booking:** Required, book now (peak July). Online at marejoseavillez.pt → Reservations, or call **+351 916 001 527**.

### Arola (Penha Longa) — backup
Sergi Arola tapas, shares a kitchen with Michelin-starred LAB.

**Top 3 (non-shrimp):**
1. **Smoked ham "5J"** — crystal bread, tomato, olive oil
2. **Potato "Bravas"** — double-cooked, spicy sauce, aioli (signature)
3. **Cod Buñuelos** — fritters, garlic tempura, lemon mayo

**⚠️ Avoid:** Shrimp Salad (spinach, yuzu, parmesan, cassava chips), Lobster/Seafood Rice.
**Vegetarian:** Explicitly vegetarian/vegan/gluten-free friendly. Burrata with cherry tomato & olives is a clean veg pick; kitchen accommodates requests.
**Booking:** You're staying on-property — book via Penha Longa concierge, or online via TheFork (live availability confirmed), or **+351 219 249 011**.

## Algarve (all on-resort, Pine Cliffs)

### Rohini by Little Miss India — Jul 26 PICK ⚠️ confirm operating
Authentic Indian with a Goan-Portuguese lean.

**Top 3 (non-shrimp, very low-risk menu overall):**
1. **Dhaba Murgh** — robust chicken curry, onion-tomato sauce
2. **Goan Fish Curry** or **Ajawani Mahi Tikka** (boneless fish, yogurt-spice marinade)
3. **Hara Bhara Kebab** — pan-fried spinach & pea patty, stuffed with cheese (**VEGETARIAN**)

No shrimp dishes turned up in any menu coverage — genuinely the safest restaurant on the list for the allergy.
**Vegetarian:** Strong. Onion Bhaji, Tandoor broccoli "Malai Ke Phool," Hara Bhara Kebab, Malai Ka Motichur (dessert) all vegetarian.
**Booking:** Via pinecliffs.com/dining/rohini or **+351 289 500 100**. **Call first to confirm it's actually open Jul 26** (see flag above).

### Yakuza Algarve by Olivier — Jul 27 PICK ⚠️ confirm operating
Japanese fusion, open-air zen garden, dinner only.

**Top 3 (non-shrimp, verified via official site):**
1. **Kobe's Picanha** — signature Olivier's-brand steak dish
2. **Olivier's steak** — robata-grilled
3. **Linguini with Black Truffle** — pasta, likely vegetarian-adjacent (confirm no hidden meat stock)

**⚠️ Avoid:** Any "ebi" (shrimp) nigiri, tempura, or shrimp gyoza if offered on the sushi side.
**Vegetarian:** Plenty of vegan + gluten-free marked dishes (edamame, wakame salad, harumaki, vegan sushi combo).
**Booking:** TheFork (live availability) or pinecliffs.com/dining → Book now, or **+351 934 000 359**. **Confirm it's open before relying on it** (see flag above).

### O Pescador — Jul 28 PICK
Algarvian fine-dining seafood, pick-your-fish-from-the-display format.

**Top 3 (non-shrimp):**
1. **Tuna Tartare** — the standout starter across multiple reviews
2. **Grilled catch of the day** — pick fish from the display (robalo/sole/turbot), grilled or salt-crust
3. **Grilled octopus** — repeatedly called out as tender, well-executed

**⚠️ Avoid:** Shrimp risotto, seafood rice, cataplana — all very likely to contain shrimp.
**Note:** Ria Formosa oysters are mollusks (separate allergy category), generally lower risk but confirm.
**Vegetarian:** Weak — heavily seafood-focused; green bean tempura is the one veg side spotted. Call ahead.
**Booking:** pinecliffs.com/dining or **+351 289 500 100**, or via resort concierge.

### Maré at Pine Cliffs — Jul 29 PICK ⚠️ confirm hours/walk-in
Beach-club style, casual grilled/seafood menu.

**Top 3 (non-shrimp):**
1. **Grilled salmon** (Salmão Grelhado) — most-reviewed dish on the menu
2. **Frango Piri Piri** — piri piri chicken
3. **Grilled squid salad** — fresh vegetables, lemon vinaigrette

**⚠️ Avoid:** Garlic Prawns, Shrimp Mayo Wrap, Prawn-based sides — all explicitly on the menu.
**Vegetarian:** Caesar/Caprese salads available, limited beyond that.
**Booking:** Per a detailed review, **this venue does not take reservations** — walk-in and expect a wait. Confirm hours with concierge before counting on it for dinner (see flag above).

### Piri Piri Steak House — open 5th-night option
Prime steaks from around the world, Portuguese wine pairings. Open Wed–Sat 6:30–10:30pm only.

Inherently low shrimp-risk (steakhouse). Didn't get full dish-level detail in research — if you want this firmed up before booking, I can do another pass.
**Booking:** **+351 289 500 100** or via concierge. Note the limited open days — only Wed/Thu/Fri/Sat, so it only works if your 5th Algarve night lands on one of those.

## Lisbon

### Bairro do Avillez – Páteo — Jul 31 PICK (if chosen over Prado)
Seafood-and-meat sharing plates, José Avillez's Chiado food-hall. *(Photo-verified directly from the restaurant's own site — these are confirmed real menu items, not generic guesses.)*

**Top 3 (non-shrimp/crustacean, photo-confirmed):**
1. **Ameijoas à Bulhão Pato** — clams, garlic, olive oil, coriander (mollusk, separate allergy category from shrimp)
2. **Favinhas com chouriço** — fava beans with chorizo, fully shellfish-free
3. **Robalo grelhado** — grilled sea bass (ask the kitchen to swap the shrimp-rice side it's normally plated with for something else)

**⚠️ Avoid:** Açorda de camarão (shrimp bread soup), Lavagante com arroz (lobster rice), the signature mixed seafood rice (lobster, prawns, shrimp, clams), Stuffed Spider Crab.
**Vegetarian:** Mushroom Risotto with Basil; Burrata with watercress.
**Booking:** Reservations a must per multiple reviews, especially for a group of 9. Direct online: sevenrooms.com/reservations/pateobairrodoavillez

### Prado — backup, strongest veg option in Lisbon
Farm-to-table, Michelin-recognized, menu changes daily/seasonally.

**Top 3 (no shrimp found anywhere in menu research — lowest-risk restaurant on the list):**
1. **Mirandesa Beef Tartare** — shiitake, grilled cabbage
2. **Iberico pork tenderloin** — quinces, chard
3. **Hispi cabbage** — goat's cheese whey, walnuts (**VEGETARIAN**, signature)

**Vegetarian:** Explicitly "many vegetarian options" (Yelp), seasonal plant-forward menu, not a token gesture — genuinely the strongest veg pick of the whole trip.
**Booking:** Required, small space, popular. Via pradorestaurante.com or **+351 21 053 4649**. À la carte available at all sittings (not just tasting menu) — fits your no-tasting-menu rule.

### Ibo — Aug 1 PICK ✅ confirmed open
Mozambican-Goan riverside, Cais do Sodré, est. 2008, chef João Pedrosa. *(Confirmed live and active: TheFork shows same-day bookings, 1,163 reviews at 9.3/10, current hours Tue–Sun 12–9pm listed on Portugal's official restaurant association registry.)*

**Top 3 (non-shrimp):**
1. **Fish curry in coconut & coriander sauce** (Caril de peixe) — fish-based, no shellfish
2. **Chacuti de cabrito** (baby goat curry, €24) — meat, no shellfish
3. **Stuffed mushrooms** with goat cheese & Bellota ham, or **spinach "Tentúgal"** with sherry tomato & arugula (starters, no shellfish)

**⚠️ Avoid — this one needs real care:** Ibo's most-praised, most-ordered dishes are shrimp-built: **wild prawns "the Laurentina way"** (the single most-recommended dish across every review), **grilled tiger prawns piri piri**, **gambas in filo pastry**, crab salad with mango. This is the highest-shrimp-density menu on the whole trip. Call ahead and flag the allergy explicitly when booking.
**Vegetarian:** Limited — one reviewer noted "only one vegetarian main, but it's worth it." Ask the server directly; the spinach starter and stuffed mushrooms are safe veg picks.
**Booking:** Required, popular riverside spot, 42-seat dining room + 16-seat terrace. Via TheFork (live availability, "7 bookings today") or **+351 913 862 956**.
